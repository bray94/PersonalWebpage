Inspired by Nature

During my senior year of college, a few friends and I started a solar company called Inpried by Nature. Our goal with the company is to get solar to those who can't afford it or who don't really know about it by renting their space for the use of producing solar energy. We are working to bring solar to a whole different area of the population that has traditionally been left out by making the process to get solar as easy as possible. We are also working to observe and map out an area for solar through apps as to expediate the process. We have gotten a little bit of funding and went through the interview process with world renown Y Combinator. My job for Inspired by Nature is Chief Technology Officer. I have been tasked with creating the wesbite, www.ibnhomes.com, and handling the software/technology that our company needs. I taught myself web programming and how to create a website from scratch, as well as turn an old computer in to a server to host our website. We built a tiered solar structure for use on rowhomes to maximize profit in the smaller areas. This structure was designed to be self cleaning/cooling from collecting rain water. This startup has forced me to learn a lot in a short amount of time and realize what it really takes to take a project like this to market.
